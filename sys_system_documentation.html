5. If the status is not triage, go to screen '508 - Laptop Data - Update New Values' and change the status to build
6. Get back to screen '200 - Build - Update from LSHW'

Sales - Sales Processing for Laptop Crew

Functionality:
1. Situation 1: This process was developed to take off a laptop from the list of laptops for sale. It is a 'work around' where sales processing in LIS is not yet done by the 'front desk' staff. (If the sales process is done in LIS, the laptop is automatically taken off the list of laptops for sale and this 'work around' is not required.)
2. Situation 2: A laptop is shown on the list of laptops for sale but is not located among the laptops on the shelves/inventory. In most cases, the laptop was taken by a laptop crew to the 'front desk' for sales processing but did not update the laptop status using this 'work around'. In order to take it off the list of laptops for sale, this 'work around' is required.
Note: In the future this 'work around' can be implemented for internal control purposes in that the accountability of the laptop is transferred from the 'laptop room' to the 'front desk'.
3. Functionality:
3.1 Change status of laptop to '4salesproc' (4salesproc = turnover the custody of the laptop from the 'laptop room' to 'front desk' staff for sales processing)
3.2 The laptop should no longer appear on the list of laptops for sales (process 550)

Prerequisites:
1. The laptop status must be 'pricinga'

General Process:
1. Use process '438 - Sales: Update for Sales Processing' (template)
2. Select the SKU from the dropdown list
3. Click 'Submit' button
4. The laptop should no longer appear on the list of laptops for sales (process 550)
5. If this is a sale on the spot, pysically carry the laptop to the 'front desk' for issuance of sales receipt and processing of payments
